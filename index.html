Channel-Specific Guidelines

Email Communications
Subject Lines: 6-10 words, benefit-focused
Opening: State purpose in first sentence
Body: Use bullets for multiple points
CTA: Single, clear action within 100 words
Subject: "Your Q3 metrics + 3 action items"
Opening: "Hi Sarah, your report is ready with three insights that need immediate attention."
Blog Posts
Hook: Problem or question in first 50 words
Structure: Subheadings every 2-3 paragraphs
Evidence: Data point or example per section
Conclusion: Actionable takeaways + next steps
Opening: "Did you know 67% of marketers struggle with consistency? Here's the framework that changed everything for us."
Social Media
Opening: Hook with question or statistic
Middle: Value proposition clearly stated
Emojis: 1-2 maximum, purposefully placed
Closing: Engagement prompt or question
"Ever wonder why some content goes viral?
We analyzed 10,000 posts and found 3 patterns...
